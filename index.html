Kurv

A world of pies!

Our pie motto? Grab the world by the rolling pins and make magic! Each day our kitchen is filled with the scent of freshly baked pie floating out the door to the inner city streets of Copenhagen. We specialize in authentic American-style pies made from scratch using simple quality ingredients. We roll out each pie crust and peel every apple by hand. From roasting chicken, to stirring lemon curd, our pies are made with a lot of hard work and a lot of care.
